LatestProject!

VisionMate

Developed VisionMate, an innovative digital wellness solution aimed at combating eye strain and reducing the risk of myopia associated with excessive screen time. The application implements the 20-20-20 eye-care principle, encouraging users to look at an object 20 feet away for 20 seconds after every 20 minutes of screen usage through automated reminders and intelligent background monitoring. By transforming a clinically recommended practice into an engaging and reliable mobile experience, VisionMate promotes healthier digital habits, preventive eye care, and long-term visual well-being..

Air Quality Monitoring System

Conceptualized and developed an intelligent Air Quality Monitoring System that captures, processes, and visualizes real-time environmental data, including Air Quality Index (AQI), temperature, and humidity for indoor environments. The platform delivers actionable insights through live monitoring, data analytics, and automated alerts, promoting environmental awareness and proactive decision-making. Recognized for its innovation, technical excellence, and potential social impact, the project was awarded NGIN funding and secured 2nd Prize at the TIT College Hackathon, competing against multiple student-led innovations.

HIVInsight

HIVInsight is a user-friendly mobile application designed to provide easy access to HIV testing information and resources. It helps users locate nearby testing centers, understand the testing process, receive test results, and access educational materials about HIV prevention and treatment. The app aims to make HIV testing more accessible and convenient, promoting early detection and informed decision-making..
